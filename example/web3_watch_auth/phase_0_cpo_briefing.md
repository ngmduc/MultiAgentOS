# Phase 0: CPO Briefing - Web3 Luxury Watch Auth

## 1. Product Briefing & CPO Assessment
**Project:** dApp for Luxury Watch Provenance (Digital Passport).
**Core Mission:** Killing the "Super Clone" market by making Physical-to-Digital binding inseparable.

### The "Creative-First" Assessment:
Luxury watch certificates are pieces of paper. Paper is easily faked. We are building a **"Digital Birth Certificate"** on-chain. The watch isn't just a physical object; it's a node in a trust network.

### Key Hooks (LTV/CAC Efficiency):
- **NFC-to-Chain Bridge:** A tiny NFC tag embedded in the warranty card or watch case that triggers a "Provenance Check" on a mobile phone.
- **Transfer-on-Sale:** Ownership transfer of the NFT occurs only when the physical watch is verified by a 3rd party or escrow.
- **Service-Log NFTs:** Every authorized service/repair adds an immutable "Update" to the watch's digital history.

## 2. Raw Data Audit Trail (Research)
- **Reddit/Hacker News Insights:** "Is this Rolex real?" is the #1 question on r/watches. "Super Clones" are now 99.9% identical to the eye.
- **Competitors:** Arianee (B2B focused, slow adoption), Aura Consortium (LVMH-only, closed ecosystem).
- **Market Gap:** An independent "B2C-First" verification platform that works across all brands.

## 3. High-Level Requirements
- **Swarm Activation:**
    - **CTO:** Choose a Low-Gas/L2 blockchain (Polygon or Base) for cost-effective minting.
    - **CMO:** Target "Gray Market" dealers and "High-Net-Worth Collector" communities.
    - **COO:** "Trust Fee" per transaction on the secondary market.

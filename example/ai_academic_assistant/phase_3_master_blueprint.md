# Phase 3: Master Blueprint - AI Academic Writing Assistant (ScholarShield)

## 🏆 Only Viable Product (OVP)
A citation-aware AI writing assistant that enforces a **"Strict Source Anchor"** rule: no claim is generated unless it is explicitly linked to a verified academic PDF in the user's private or public library.

## 🚀 Killer Sales Points (The "Hooks")
1.  **"Zero-Hallucination" Mode:** Our RAG engine fails-safe. If no source exists in the database to support a claim, the AI explicitly flags "Insufficient Data" rather than inventing a fact.
2.  **Submit-Ready Citations:** Automatically formats citations (APA, MLA, Harvard) based on the *actual* structural metadata of the linked PDF, not just a guessed title.
3.  **The "Integrity Audit":** A one-click scan of an entire draft that flags unverifiable claims, potentially preventing embarrassing retractions or peer-review failures.

## 💰 Valuation Vision & Economics
- **Target LTV:** $5,000+ (Institutional contracts for university departments).
- **Target CAC:** $20 via academic Twitter and SEO for "how to fix AI hallucinations in research."
- **Exit Strategy:** Acquisition by Elsevier, Overleaf, or Clarivate (Web of Science).

## 📅 Roadmap (The "Sprints")
- **W1-2:** Semantic Scholar API integration + Pinecone Vector Store setup.
- **W3:** Hybrid PDF parsing engine (Textract + Grobid citation extraction).
- **W4:** "Integrity Audit" UI + Beta launch for 10 university labs.

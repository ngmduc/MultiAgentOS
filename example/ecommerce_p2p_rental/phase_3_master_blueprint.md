A high-trust P2P gear rental marketplace that solves **Voluntary Parting Theft** through a mandatory "AI-Assist" photo-verification loop and a tiered trust system based on rental history.

## 🚀 Killer Sales Points (The "Hooks")
1.  **"Bulletproof" Insurance:** The only platform that explicitly covers 'Voluntary Parting' (renter disappearance) without a $2,000 deductible.

3.  **The "Elite-Verified" Tier:** High-end gear (RED, ARRI) is only visible and rentable to users with a verified identity and a $5k+ "Trust Score" from previous rentals.

# Phase 0: CPO Briefing - P2P Gear Rental Marketplace

## 1. Product Briefing & CPO Assessment
**Project:** P2P Rental for High-End Photo/Video Gear.
**Core Mission:** Decouple equipment ownership from creative production by solving the "Trust Gap."

### The "Creative-First" Assessment:
Current P2P marketplaces (ShareGrid, KitSplit) are essentially listing sites. The pain isn't finding gear; it's the anxiety of "Voluntary Parting" (theft by deception) and logistical friction. We aren't building a directory; we're building an **Insurance and Verification Engine** that happens to have a marketplace UI.

### Key Hooks (LTV/CAC Efficiency):
- **Instant "Voluntary Parting" Coverage:** Built-in insurance that covers the specific loophole traditional policies miss.
- **Biometric Handover:** QR-code + FaceID verification at the moment of physical hand-off to prevent "ghosting."
- **Asset Health Logs:** AI-assisted pre/post rental photo verification to automate damage claims.

## 2. Raw Data Audit Trail (Research)
- **Reddit/Hacker News Insights:** "Voluntary parting" is the #1 reason owners pull gear. scheduling friction and "ghosting" are the #2.
- **Competitors:** Lensrentals (Centralized, expensive shipping), ShareGrid (Fragmented, insurance is a friction point).
- **Market Gap:** A platform that treats gear like a high-yield financial asset for owners and a frictionless utility for renters.

## 3. High-Level Requirements
- **Swarm Activation:**
    - **CTO:** Design a stateless "Handover Protocol" using mobile app biometric verification.
    - **CMO:** Target "Indie Filmmakers" and "Wedding Photographers"—high frequency, high trust needs.
    - **COO:** Design the payout-to-risk model; reduce Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) to <24h post-return.

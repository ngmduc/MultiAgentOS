# Phase 2: Contrarian Debate - B2B SaaS CRM for Designers

## 🥊 Round 1: Cross-Examination (Bắn Tỉa Lẫn Nhau)

**CTO attacks CMO:**
"CMO proposes 'Zero-Dollar Acquisition' via free templates. This is a technical liability. If we attract thousands of 'template-seekers' who never convert, our S3 and compute costs for the 'Spatial Feedback' engine will spike without revenue. How do we gate the high-cost features without killing the viral hook?"

**COO attacks CTO:**
"CTO wants a 'Spatial Feedback' engine with deep Figma integration. This sounds like an engineering money pit. Building a stable, real-time spatial comment system for high-res assets is a 6-month dev cycle. We need an MVP in 4 weeks. Can we just use a 'screenshot-and-ping' method first to stay under budget?"

**CMO attacks COO:**
"COO suggests a 'Pay When You Get Paid' (2%) model. This is terrible for branding. It makes us look like a 'Payment Processor' (Stripe/PayPal), not a 'Premium Creative Partner.' High-end designers want to feel like they are paying for a professional tool, not a commission-based middleman. Why not a 'Success Fee' instead?"

**CPO Direction:**
"CTO is right about infrastructure cost; we need a hard limit on free storage. COO is right about speed; focus on the Figma iframe/metadata link rather than rebuilding a canvas. CMO, defend the brand—how do we make $20/mo feel like an investment, not a cost?"

---

## 🛡️ Round 2: Defense & Pivot (Bảo Vệ & Cải Tiến)

**CTO Defense:**
"I will pivot the architecture. Instead of internal S3 hosting for everything, we use the **'Figma-Link' approach**. The CRM stores the *pointer* to the Figma file. We only render high-fidelity snapshots for the 'Feedback' loop. This cuts storage costs by 90% and development time by half. We use the Figma API to 'pull' comments into our UI."

**CMO Defense:**
"To address COO's branding concern: we pivot to a **'Hybrid Professional' Tier**. $29/mo flat fee, but we offer 'Invoice Financing' as an add-on. This makes us the 'Bank of the Designer' without the 'Cheap Processor' vibe. We use the 'Client Portal' as a high-end status symbol—it looks better than a Figma link."

**COO Defense:**
"Accepting the CTO's leaner dev path. If we launch in 4 weeks, we can burn less cash. I will change the model to a **'Value-Based Tier'**: Free for 1 active project, $29/mo for unlimited. This gates the infrastructure cost while letting the 'Template-seekers' use the tool once."

---

## ✨ Round 3: Final Polish (Mài Giũa Cuối Cùng)

**Final Alignment:**
- **Tech:** A 'Metadata-Light' CRM that links to Figma/Adobe, not a storage cloud.
- **Marketing:** The hook is "Feedback-to-Invoice" automation. "Stop the email chase."
- **Commercials:** $29/mo flat fee with a 'Project-Limited' free tier to drive virality.

**CPO Conclusion:**
"The strategy is now lean and punchy. We are the 'Automation Layer' between the design tool and the client's wallet. Proceed to Phase 3 Master Blueprint."

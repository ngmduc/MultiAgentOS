# Phase 3: Master Blueprint - B2B SaaS CRM (CreativeFlow)

## 🏆 Only Viable Product (OVP)
A "Metadata-Light" CRM specifically for freelance designers that automates the transition from **Figma Feedback to Final Invoice**. It doesn't store files; it manages the *relationship* and the *financial triggers* of a design project.

## 🚀 Killer Sales Points (The "Hooks")
1.  **Figma-to-Task Link:** Automatically converts Figma comments into actionable CRM project tasks without leaving the design tool.
2.  **The "Silent Negotiator" Portal:** A premium client portal that timestamps every feedback round, automatically flagging "Limit Reached" to prevent scope-creep.
3.  **One-Click Wrap-Up:** When the "Approval" button is hit in the portal, a final invoice is instantly generated and sent via Stripe, tethering the files to the payment.

## 💰 Valuation Vision & Economics
- **Target LTV:** $1,200 (Retention of 3.5 years at $29/mo).
- **Target CAC:** < $80 via organic "Design Audit" templates and "Powered by CreativeFlow" branding on portals.
- **Exit Strategy:** Acquisition by Figma or HubSpot as their "Vertical Design" arm.

## 📅 Roadmap (The "Sprints")
- **W1-2:** Core Graph DB for Project/Task linking + Figma API integration.
- **W3:** "Client Portal" MVP for feedback collection.
- **W4:** Stripe/Invoice integration + Beta Launch for 50 designers.

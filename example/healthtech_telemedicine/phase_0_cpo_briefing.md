# Phase 0: CPO Briefing - HealthTech Telemedicine for Entrepreneurs

## 1. Product Briefing & CPO Assessment
**Project:** Mental Health Platform for Startup Founders.
**Core Mission:** Reducing "Founder Burnout" through specialized, high-intensity support.

### The "Creative-First" Assessment:
Standard therapy (BetterHelp) is too "general" for founders dealing with Term Sheets, Layoffs, and Cap Table stress. Founders need **"Peer-Shield" support**—therapists who have actually been in the trenches or understand the unique psychology of high-growth scaling.

### Key Hooks (LTV/CAC Efficiency):
- **Crisis-Mode Button:** 15-minute "Emergency Vent" session before a Board Meeting or Layoff.
- **Founder Circles:** Anonymous 4-person peer groups led by a psychologist.
- **Burnout Predictor:** Integrates with Slack/Calendar (with privacy) to flag signs of mental fatigue based on work patterns.

## 2. Raw Data Audit Trail (Research)
- **Reddit/Hacker News Insights:** "My therapist doesn't understand why I can't just 'take a weekend off'." "I'm lonely at the top."
- **Competitors:** BetterHelp (Low specialized knowledge), Talkspace (Generalist).
- **Market Gap:** High-end, hyper-niche therapy for the 0.1% risk-takers.

## 3. High-Level Requirements
- **Swarm Activation:**
    - **CTO:** Build a privacy-first, HIPAA-compliant encryption layer that blocks even database admins from session logs.
    - **CMO:** Target "VC Portfolios"—get VCs to pay for their founders as a "De-risking" investment.
    - **COO:** High-margin ($200+/session) specialized professional service.

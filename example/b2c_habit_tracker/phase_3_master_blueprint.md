# Phase 3: Master Blueprint - B2C Habit Tracker (ResilienceRPG)

## 🏆 Only Viable Product (OVP)
A "Mercy-First" habit tracker that replaces the "Streak Reset" with a **Recovery Quest Engine**. It uses RPG mechanics (Avatars, Quests, HP) to gamify addiction recovery and habit building without the "shame cycle" of traditional trackers.

## 🚀 Killer Sales Points (The "Hooks")
1.  **The "Mercy" System:** If a day is missed, the streak is "Frozen," not reset. To unfreeze it, the user must complete a scientifically-backed "Recovery Challenge."
2.  **RPG Avatar Progression:** Every habit feeds a stat (e.g., "Walking" feeds "Agility"). Your avatar reflects your real-world effort, making "Progress" visible even if the streak is temporarily paused.
3.  **Local-First / E2EE Privacy:** "The Vault"—a private journaling section for addiction triggers that is encrypted on-device and never reaches the server.

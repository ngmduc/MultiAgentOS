# Phase 3: Master Blueprint - EdTech Retail Training (DuelSkills)

## 🏆 Only Viable Product (OVP)
A gamified micro-learning platform for retail staff that uses **Head-to-Head Duels** and TikTok-style video to reduce "New-Hire Time-to-Floor" and increase product knowledge.

## 🚀 Killer Sales Points (The "Hooks")
1.  **The "60-Second Duel":** Associates play 1-minute knowledge games against their peers for points and prestige. It’s training that happens during slow moments on the floor, not in a backroom.
2.  **Rapid Onboarding:** Reduces technical training time by 60% through AI-generated "Flash Modules" based on the store's current inventory.
3.  **Offline "Smart-Sync":** Ensures associates never use their own data plans; the app only syncs video content when connected to store Wi-Fi.

## 💰 Valuation Vision & Economics
- **Target LTV:** $12,000+ per regional retail contract (10-store cluster).
- **Target CAC:** $500 via District Manager outreach on LinkedIn.
- **Exit Strategy:** Acquisition by a large HCM (Human Capital Management) player like Workday or UKG.

## 📅 Roadmap (The "Sprints")
- **W1-2:** WebSocket Duel Engine + Video feed UI.
- **W3:** AI Quiz Generator (CSV to Duel) + Leaderboard logic.
- **W4:** "Store Champion" Tournament Mode + Pilot launch with 3 retail partners.

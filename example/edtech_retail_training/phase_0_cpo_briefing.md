# Phase 0: CPO Briefing - EdTech Micro-learning for Retail

## 1. Product Briefing & CPO Assessment
**Project:** 3-Minute Gamified Training for Retail Frontline.
**Core Mission:** Solving Training-at-Scale in high-turnover industries.

### The "Creative-First" Assessment:
Retail workers hate LMS (Learning Management Systems). They have 5 minutes of downtime behind a counter, not 1 hour in a back office. We are building **"TikTok for Training."** If the module takes longer than it takes to brew a coffee, we failed.

### Key Hooks (LTV/CAC Efficiency):
- **Shift-Start Challenges:** 2-minute "Daily Specials" duel between co-workers.
- **NFC-Triggered Learning:** Tap a new product tag to see its 30-second "How to Sell" video.
- **Leaderboard-to-Shift-Priority:** Gamification that actually impacts real-world perks (e.g., first pick of weekend shifts).

## 2. Raw Data Audit Trail (Research)
- **Reddit/Hacker News Insights:** Managers find traditional training "useless" because staff forget 90% by Tuesday. Frontline staff feel "disconnected" from corporate updates.
- **Competitors:** Axonify (Strong but corporate/heavy), YOOBIC (Good but complex).
- **Market Gap:** Ultra-light, mobile-first, "Duel-based" learning.

## 3. High-Level Requirements
- **Swarm Activation:**
    - **CTO:** Build a video-first mobile architecture with offline support for dead zones in malls.
    - **CMO:** Sell to "Operations Directors"—frame it as "Reducing Shrinkage" and "Increasing Upsell."
    - **COO:** Pricing should be per-store, not per-user (to account for high turnover).

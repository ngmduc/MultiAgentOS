# Phase 2: Contrarian Debate - Productivity Mind-map Task Manager

## 🥊 Round 1: Cross-Examination (Bắn Tỉa Lẫn Nhau)

**CTO attacks CMO:**
"CMO wants a 'Free Public Mind-Map Gallery.' This is a data privacy nightmare. If we let users 'Share their workflow,' they might accidentally share client names or internal architecture. This requires a 'Data Sanitization' AI that scans every public map. How do we pay for that on a free tier?"

**COO attacks CTO:**
"CTO proposes an 'Infinite Canvas' with Neo4j. This is extremely heavy on the client. If a project has 5,000 nodes, the browser's DOM or even a Canvas-based renderer will struggle to maintain 60FPS. Can we limit the 'Visible Depth' rather than showing everything at once?"

**CMO attacks COO:**
"COO suggests 'Pay-per-Contributor.' Most 'Creative Strategists' start alone. If they can't invite a client to *view* the map for free, they won't use us. We need 'Free Viewers' and 'Paid Editors.' Why are we stingy on the collaboration hook?"

**CPO Direction:**
"CTO, 'Level-of-Detail' rendering is the solution; only show nodes within 2 steps of the cursor. COO, 'Free Viewers' are a must for virality—they are our future CAC. CMO, the AI scanner for public maps is worth the cost; it's our 'Trust-Badge' for the community."

---

## 🛡️ Round 2: Defense & Pivot (Bảo Vệ & Cải Tiến)

**CTO Defense:**
"I will pivot to a **'Z-Buffer Depth Rendering' engine**. The canvas only renders the 'Active Branch' and its children in high fidelity, fading others into abstract shapes. This keeps the FPS at 60 even for 10,000+ nodes. I'll also add a 'Private Mode' that defaults every node to 'Blurred' until explicitly 'Published'."

**CMO Defense:**
"I will change the GTM to the **'Viewer-to-Editor' funnel**. Clients can view and 'Add Sticky Notes' for free. If the client wants to 'Assign a Task' or 'Edit the Graph,' the Strategist gets prompted to upgrade to a Pro Seat. This makes the Strategist our 'Internal Salesperson'."

**COO Defense:**
"Accepting the **'Free Viewer' model**. I will cap it at **3 Viewers per Map** for the free tier. For Pro Strategists ($19/mo), viewers are unlimited. This protects our margins while allowing for 'High-Trust Collaboration.' The AI scanner will use a lean, local-LLM (Llama-3-8B) to keep costs down."

---

## ✨ Round 3: Final Polish (Mài Giũa Cuối Cùng)

**Final Alignment:**
- **Tech:** 'Depth-of-Field' Canvas rendering + Local-AI privacy scanner for public maps.
- **Marketing:** The "Strategist's Workspace." Focus on 'Zero-Transition' planning.
- **Commercials:** $19/mo Pro seat with unlimited free viewers.

**CPO Conclusion:**
"The 'Missing Link' in productivity. No more 'Wall to List' friction. Proceed to Phase 3 Master Blueprint."

# Phase 3: Master Blueprint - Productivity Mind-map (StrategyMap)

## 🏆 Only Viable Product (OVP)
A unified "Graph-Task" manager that connects the high-level "Brainstorm" (Mind-map) and the low-level "Action" (Kanban) in a single, high-performance infinite canvas.

## 🚀 Killer Sales Points (The "Hooks")
1.  **"Zero-Transition" Workflow:** Every bubble in your mind-map is a live ticket. Change the status in the Kanban board, and the color of the node shifts on the map instantly.
2.  **AI Branch Expansion:** Stuck on "Marketing Strategy"? Click the AI icon on a node, and it generates a 5-step sub-branch of tasks tailored to your project.
3.  **The "Live-View" Share:** Send a link to your client; they see a read-only, interactive map of project progress. No more "Status Update" emails.

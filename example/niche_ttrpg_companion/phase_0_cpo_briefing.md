# Phase 0: CPO Briefing - Niche TTRPG Companion

## 1. Product Briefing & CPO Assessment
**Project:** Combat & Music Sync for TTRPG DMs.
**Core Mission:** Automating Immersion for the overloaded Dungeon Master.

### The "Creative-First" Assessment:
Dungeon Masters are cognitive-overloaded. They are managing 5 players, 10 monsters, and a story. Managing a Spotify playlist is the last thing they want to do. We are building an **"Atmosphere Trigger Engine."** One click on an "Initiative" button shifts the lighting (HUE) and the music (Spotify/Local) to "Combat Mode" instantly.

### Key Hooks (LTV/CAC Efficiency):
- **Moment-Based Playlists:** "Stealth," "Tense Dialogue," "Dragon Fight"—one-click transitions.
- **Monster-Action Soundboard:** Integrated sounds for spells and attacks that trigger automatically when the DM rolls.
- **Hue/LIFX Integration:** Change the real-world room lighting based on the game state.

## 2. Raw Data Audit Trail (Research)
- **Reddit/Hacker News Insights:** "I stop playing music because it's too much work to change tracks between scenes." "Syrinscape is great but the UI is from 1998."
- **Competitors:** D&D Beyond (No music), Syrinscape (Powerful but terrible UX), Roll20 (Jukebox is clunky).
- **Market Gap:** A modern, mobile-first soundboard that "listens" to the game state.

## 3. High-Level Requirements
- **Swarm Activation:**
    - **CTO:** Build an API integration with D&D Beyond (via browser extension) or a standalone combat tracker.
    - **CMO:** Focus on "Immersion Without Effort." Target the r/DMAcademy "Lazy DM" demographic.
    - **COO:** One-time purchase + Monthly content packs (New soundscapes).
